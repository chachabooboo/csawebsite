CSA Collaborates with NSCS on Let's Stand Together Campaign

29 May 2015


Started in 2013, the Let's Stand Together campaign aims to bring everyone – from companies and government agencies, to members of the public – together to protect our country, economy and people. Spearheaded by the National Security Coordination Secretariat (NSCS), in collaboration with multiple partners including CSA, the focus of this year's campaign is 21st Century Readiness, with cyber security, terrorism and pandemics as its three focus themes.


Videos and print ads have been produced for each theme, with the messages "Block the Hack", "Report the Threat" and "Stop the Spread" respectively. Look out for them in the print media, train stations and digital screens in town and the heartlands. Visit our facebook page to find out more!
