CSA Remains Operational During Period of Elevated Safe Distancing Measures

07 Apr 2020

The Cyber Security Agency of Singapore remains operational during this period of elevated safe distancing measures, between 7 April to 1 June (inclusive) 2020. 

Should you wish to report a cybersecurity incident, please do so via SingCERT’s Cyber Incident Reporting Form or email SingCERT at singcert@csa.gov.sg. 

For enquiries or feedback on CSA’s programmes and schemes, please write to us at contact@csa.gov.sg. 

For more details on public services remaining accessible during this period, you may refer to the Public Service Division's website. 

As we move our work and social activities online, please remember to stay vigilant and #gosafeonline. #SGUnited
