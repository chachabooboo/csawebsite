The Cybersecurity Awards and Gala Dinner 2018

07 Nov 2017

The inaugural Cybersecurity Awards aim to recognise individuals and enterprises which have made significant contributions to Singapore’s cybersecurity ecosystem. The Awards are organised by the Association of Information Security Professionals (AISP), and supported by CSA as well as the following professional and industry associations – Singapore Computer Society (SCS), Singapore Infocommm Technology Federation (SiTF), (ISC)2, Information Systems Audit and Control Association (ISACA), Cloud Security Alliance, the IT Service Management Forum (itSMF) and Law Society.

There are three main award categories: 1) Professionals; 2) Enterprises and 3) Students. Each award nominee will go through two rounds of judging by a panel comprising industry leaders and senior officials from CSA. The winners will receive the awards from Minister for Communications and Information and Minister-in-charge of Cyber Security Dr Yaacob Ibrahim at the inaugural Cybersecurity Gala Dinner in February 2018.

Nominations for the Cybersecurity Awards 2018 are now closed.

To find out more about sponsorship opportunities and how you can contribute to the success of The Cybersecurity Awards and Gala Dinner 2018, please visit the AISP website or contact Mr Lawrence Tan at lawrence.tan@aisp.sg or 9815 0830.
