CSA and DSO to Work Together to Strengthen Critical Information Infrastructure Protection

19 Jan 2017


Mr David Koh, Chief Executive of CSA with Mr Cheong Chee Hoo, Chief Executive Officer of DSO National Laboratories at the  MOU Signing Ceremony

The Cyber Security Agency of Singapore (CSA) signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) with DSO National Laboratories on 18 January 2017. The MOU was signed by Mr David Koh, Chief Executive of the CSA and Mr Cheong Chee Hoo, Chief Executive Officer of DSO National Laboratories. The MOU will strengthen cooperation in cybersecurity issues and enhance efforts towards safeguarding our Critical Information Infrastructure in the interests of national security.
