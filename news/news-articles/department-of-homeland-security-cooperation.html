CSA and the National Research Foundation (NRF) Signs Project Agreement with Department of Homeland Security (DHS) of the US Government

08 Dec 2015



CSA and the National Research Foundation (NRF) jointly entered into a Cyber Security Science & Technology co-operation with the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) of the US Government. The Project Agreement (PA) was co-signed by Dr Robert Griffin, Deputy Under Secretary - DHS, Mr David Koh,  CE CSA, and Prof Low Teck Seng, CEO NRF this morning at MCI.  It aims to foster cooperation on cybersecurity S&T through coordinated research and development, joint activities and information exchange. This PA was signed under the aegis of the Master Agreement on S&T cooperation for homeland/domestic security matters signed between the Government of Singapore and the US Government in 2007.
