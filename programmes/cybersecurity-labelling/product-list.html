Cybersecurity Labelling Scheme (CLS)
Product List

This page lists the products registered under the CLS.

The product’s registration ID is in the format: CSA/Date of Label Expiration in DDMMYY/Product ID number assigned by CSA. The validity of the label is the period for which the manufacturers will support the device with security updates, up to a maximum of a period of 3 years.

If you come across a product that is not listed on this page but bears the CLS label, please alert us immediately at certification@csa.gov.sg.
