SG Cyber Women

Programme Description

 As part of Singapore’s efforts to build the cybersecurity talent pipeline, CSA has worked with industry partners to develop the SG Cyber Women (#SGCyberWomen) initiative, a targeted initiative to leverage on the under-represented talent pool by encouraging more females, from as young as pre-tertiary age, to join the cybersecurity profession.

Under the initiative, CSA will coordinate and support the community’s efforts from outreach to skills development, to achieve the following objectives:

  • Engage women through education and community engagement
  • Develop their professional skillsets through learning and training
  • Advance their cybersecurity career through support and inspiration

Join our ‘SG Cyber Women X’ Series to celebrate International Women in Cyber Day 2020

As part of our efforts in supporting the International Women in Cyber Day 2020, CSA has brought together various associations and community partners to organise a series of virtual events. This ranges from technical workshops and Capture-The-Flag challenges, to career-sharing sessions, so as to encourage more women to join the cybersecurity profession. Join us and discover how diverse and dynamic the cybersecurity field can be by registering here: go.gov.sg/sgcyberwomenx0920.

Visit our partners below to participate in other upcoming events:

  • AiSP (Association of Information Security Professionals)
  • Div0 (Division Zero) Women In Cybersecurity
  • ICE71 (Innovation Cybersecurity Ecosystem 71)
  • ISACA SheLeadsTech
  • SCS (Singapore Computer Society)
  • WoSEC (Women of Security) / Women in Cyber
