How We Recruit

At CSA, we are always on the lookout for talented individuals looking to make an impact in Singapore’s cybersecurity industry.

If you are a cybersecurity enthusiast who loves working together with bright minds committed to protecting Singapore’s cyberspace, CSA could be your next adventure!

Our Recruitment Process

At CSA, we are passionate about our people and we want to know if you will be a good fit to our CSA Family by knowing you better and understanding how you work! 

As part of the recruitment process, you may be asked to undergo assessments or make presentations on a topic relevant to the role you have applied to. Use these opportunities to highlight your past accomplishments and what you can bring to the role.

Got an interview lined up? Here’s some tips on how to prepare for your interview with us:

  • Find out more about the role that you are applying for.
  • Do your research and be updated on our latest announcements and initiatives. Check out our social media channels to keep abreast of what we do.  
  • Prepare responses to common interview questions.
  • During the interview, sell yourself and show us how passionate you are about the role. Share with us your past accomplishments and how you feel your experiences and abilities will contribute to the team and organisation. 
  • We may ask you to tell us how you have encountered a difficult situation at work and the ways you dealt with it. We want to know how we can tap on your strengths and better support your weaknesses to nurture you as you build your career at CSA. 
  • Most importantly, prepare your questions in advance and don’t be afraid to ask us questions!
