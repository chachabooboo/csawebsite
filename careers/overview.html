Careers at CSA 

CSA is looking for dynamic individuals to shape, transform and safeguard our cyber landscape. As a cybersecurity professional, you will contribute to the building of a resilient and trusted cyber environment.

We have a range of exciting career opportunities at CSA across various technical, operational and policy roles, in the following areas:

  • National Cyber Resilience Group
  • Capability and Ecosystem Development Group
  • Strategy and Corporate Development Group

Find out more about these roles in our Career Guide [PDF, 5MB].

 

If you’re keen on joining us at CSA to defend our cyberspace, apply now at our Job Opportunities page!
