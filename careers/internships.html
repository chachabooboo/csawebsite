Internships

As an intern with CSA, you will get opportunities to work on challenging real-world projects under the guidance of our experienced cybersecurity practitioners.

You will also get a first-hand experience of CSA’s operations, go on learning journeys and participate in thought-provoking dialogues with our senior management.

Outstanding interns may also be recommended for an exciting career with CSA.

You may apply via your institution's internship exercise (if applicable), or directly to csa_recruit@csa.gov.sg with the following details:

  • Your area of interest
  • Your latest resume/CV
  • Education details – latest polytechnic/university results and transcripts
  • Duration and period available for internship

Only shortlisted applicants will be notified.
