News & Events

Past Events

Cyber Savvy Machine Pop-Up 

Date: 13 – 28 Jan 2020
Time: 9am – 9pm daily
Venue: Jalan Besar Community Club, 69 Jellicoe Road, Singapore 208737

To raise cybersecurity awareness, CSA introduced the Cyber Savvy Machine Pop-Up at different libraries and locations from from November 2018 to January 2020. It features the Cyber Savvy Machine, a vending machine which dispenses mini gifts to participants who answer five cybersecurity-related questions correctly.  

Go Safe Online C.A.F.E. (Cybersecurity Awareness For Everyone) Roadshows

Date: 23 – 24 November 2019
Time: 11am – 6pm daily
Venue: Our Tampines Hub, Festive Walk East (1 Tampines Walk, Singapore 528523)

The Go Safe Online C.A.F.E. (Cybersecurity Awareness For Everyone) roadshows are part of the ‘Go Safe Online’ cybersecurity awareness campaign brought to you by CSA. The campaign aims to provide useful cyber tips and information to enable internet users to better safeguard their digital assets. The campaign was launched at Ang Mo Kio Central Stage on Saturday, 14 September 2019. The roadshows feature three interactive activities where visitors can learn key cybersecurity habits to help them stay safe online. 

Cybersecurity Awareness for Everyone (CAFÉ)


Date: 5 – 6 May 2018

The Cybersecurity Awareness For Everyone (CAFÉ) is part of the ‘Cyber Tips 4 You’ cybersecurity awareness campaign brought to you by CSA to provide useful tips and information to enable internet users to better safeguard their digital assets. The campaign was launched by Guest-of-Honour, Dr Janil Puthucheary, Senior Minister of State, Ministry of Communications and Information at Bedok Mall on Saturday, 5 May 2018. The two-day event taught visitors first-hand how to create a strong and memorable password and the importance of using anti-virus software.


'Live Savvy with Cybersecurity Roadshow

Date: 11 – 12 February 2017

The ‘Live Savvy with Cybersecurity’ awareness campaign is an initiative of the Cyber Security Awareness Alliance and organised by CSA. The campaign aims to spread the message about the importance of protecting ourselves online. The campaign was launched by Guest-of-Honour, Minister for Communications and Information and Minister-in-Charge of Cyber Security, Dr Yaacob Ibrahim at the ‘Live Savvy with Cybersecurity’ Roadshow at the Toa Payoh Town Hub Atrium on Saturday, 11 February 2017. The two-day roadshow featured six activity zones where visitors could learn about cybersecurity in a fun and engaging way.

National Security Conference 2016: Fight the Dark Side

Date: 7 November 2016

The 12th edition of National Security Conference 2016 was organised by Singapore Business Federation, supported by the National Security Coordination Secretariat and CSA. The conference brought together experts from a broad cross-section of the business community to share security strategies for businesses through case studies and personal experiences, and offered valuable guidance for companies to enhance their business resilience journey.

Launch of Employee Cyber Security Kit

Date: 27 October 2015

The new Employee Cyber Security Kit aims to make it easier for businesses to achieve a structured employee education programme with minimal time, investment and manpower. 

The kit was unveiled on 27 October 2015 at the National Security Conference organised by the Singapore Business Federation (SBF) and supported by CSA and the National Security Coordination Secretariat (NSCS).  Dr Yaacob Ibrahim, Minister for Communications and Information and Minister-in-Charge of Cyber Security was the Guest-of-Honour at the event.

An initiative by NSCS and supported by CSA and SBF, the plug-and-play digital toolkit features a simple quiz which provides an initial assessment of a company’s cyber security readiness and follows up with a recommended cyber security education programme tailored to meet business needs. Included in the kit are editable presentation materials, communication tools such as digital banners, stickers and a range of resources that companies can tap on.
