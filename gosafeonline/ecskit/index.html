Did you know that the weakest link in a company’s cyber-security is the employees?

This FREE kit offers easy ways to inform, educate and remind your colleagues why they play such a big role in keeping the company safe from cyber threats. Get started by browsing these easy-to-use tools.

 

Introduction

A truly robust cybersecure business environment has to be complemented with employee education. Employee education is important as employees are the last line of defence for a company’s cyber risk efforts. They must be empowered to identify risks associated with the use of technology.

Not all companies can afford the time and the manpower required to develop an employee education programme. Hence, this Employee Cybersecurity Kit is designed for companies that see the value in cybersecurity employee education but face challenges in starting the programme.

This kit features a simple analytical tool to perform an initial assessment of your company’s cybersecurity readiness and follows up with a recommended cybersecurity education programme that is tailored to meet these needs.

We hope this kit proves useful in your efforts to build an employee cybersecurity culture in your company.
