Students

Internet safety isn’t just about what your parents or teachers tell you to do, but what you can do to stay safe while having fun online, be it chatting with your friends, posting a video that you’ve made or a song that you’ve written, or simply surfing stuff you’re interested in and checking out the latest trends.

Internet safety is a lot more than just ensuring that your computer has the latest anti-virus and firewall software installed. It’s about practising good cyber hygiene habits and not falling prey to online scams.

As a start, find out more about how to stay safe online with these tips.
