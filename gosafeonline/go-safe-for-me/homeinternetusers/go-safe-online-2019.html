Go Safe Online 2019

by Gosafeonline | 31 December 2019

Prevention is key in the fight against cyber threats. Just as we lock our doors to keep burglars out, we should practise good cyber hygiene to keep our devices and information safe from cyber criminals.

‘Go Safe Online’ is a cybersecurity awareness campaign brought to you by the Cyber Security Agency of Singapore (CSA). The campaign focuses on the personal consequences of not adopting good cybersecurity practices, as well as the four basic cyber hygiene habits that internet users should take to safeguard their digital assets.

As part of the campaign, CSA is organising a series of roadshows where visitors can get hands-on practice with key cybersecurity habits that help them stay safe online. Titled Go Safe Online C.A.F.E. (Cybersecurity Awareness For Everyone), the first of the roadshows took place on 14 and 15 September 2019 at Ang Mo Kio Central Stage, from 11am to 6pm.

The second roadshow was held at Our Tampines Hub on 23 and 24 November 2019.

For more updates, follow us on Facebook.
