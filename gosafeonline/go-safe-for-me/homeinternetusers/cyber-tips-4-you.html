Cyber Tips 4 You

by Gosafeonline | 23 April 2018

Cyber threats are growing in frequency and sophistication by the day and while Singapore is regarded as a safe country, the same cannot be said of the world wide web. Prevention is key in the fight against cyber threats and practising good cyber hygiene will ensure that we keep our devices and information safe.

‘Cyber Tips 4 You’ is a cybersecurity awareness campaign brought to you by the Cyber Security Agency of Singapore (CSA). The campaign aims to provide useful tips and information to enable internet users to better safeguard their digital assets. By doing so, we can do our part to create a safer cyberspace not just for ourselves but for everyone too!

As part of the campaign, CSA organised Cybersecurity Awareness For Everyone (CAFÉ), a two-day event where visitors learnt first-hand how to create a strong and memorable password and the importance of using anti-virus software. The event was held on 5 and 6 May at Bedok Mall.
