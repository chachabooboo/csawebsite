Parents

The Internet offers an exciting world of experiences for children and the whole family. It can be entertaining, educational and rewarding. However, using the Internet also involves risks and challenges. Children may unknowingly share personal information without realising that they may be subject to identity theft, or post content on social networks that can make them the target of cyber bullies. While the Internet is a trove of information and knowledge, they can also run the risk of being exposed to inappropriate or undesirable content that is unsafe, violent or inappropriate for their age.

As a start, you can first find out more about how to keep your children safe in the age of the Internet.

For more information on what you can do as a parent, you might also like to read:

  • Keep Your Child Safe on Mobile Devices
