Flyers & Brochures

by Gosafeonline |

As part of CSA’s annual cybersecurity awareness campaigns, CSA develops flyers and brochures for the public to learn how to better safeguard their data and devices. Click on the links below to learn more:

1. How to Go Safe Online – Prevention is key in the fight against cyber threats. Protect your digital assets by practising four basic cyber hygiene habits; they are (i) use an anti-virus software, (ii) use strong passwords and enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA), (iii) spot signs of phishing and (iv) update your software as soon as possible.
