Recommended Books on Cybersecurity

by Gosafeonline |

As part of the Cyber Savvy Machine Pop-Up that has been making its rounds through public libraries, CSA has collaborated with the National Library Board to develop a list of recommended books on cybersecurity. How do we identify cyber threats? What are the cybersecurity measures we need to take to protect ourselves and our data? Pick up one of these books to learn more about being cyber savvy today!
